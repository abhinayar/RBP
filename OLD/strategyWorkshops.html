We rely on our own brand, Right Brain, to inspire and guide us, so that our process and our techniques are all based on how the mind works. We base our Right Brain Strategy Development process on the following discoveries and principles:
  • We engage the entire marketing team from the very beginning. Why? Because without their involvement and participation, the process does not work.
  • Our approach is based on a whole brain perspective. Just as a person cannot function with various body parts operating according to their own agendas, so too organizations are unable to function effectively when there are artificial walls separating their various functions.

    Marketing is an integral part of the entire company. Therefore, the team that works on the brand strategy must include representation from all functions of the company.

    At different times we have included people from sales, advertising, public relations, market research, operations, legal, finance, research and development, manufacturing, human resources and the executive level in the Brand Strategy Development process.
  • We know that our clients are the experts on their businesses and that they know far more about them than we do. In fact, we find that they know far more than they realize. People have much tacit knowledge about the businesses they work in - tacit knowledge that cannot be used for business decisions until it is articulated.
  • The process begins with informal interviews that we conduct with key members of the brand team.

    We facilitate the process. In a team setting, we draw knowledge, intuition and creativity from you that you had no idea you possess.
  • We use techniques for the facilitation process that are designed to unleash natural creativity. These techniques are based on neuroscience and how the mind works. Specifically, they are inspired by our understanding of the right and left brain and how they work together.

    We conduct our sessions offsite because a fresh environment is conducive to conceptual thinking and creativity.
The heart and soul of the brand strategy that we develop together is the Brand Promise. We arrive at the Brand Promise near the end of a two-day offsite work session. Once defined, we develop examples of how to bring the promise to life in every place where the brand touches the customer.
