Emotional Segmentation

We segment consumers based on the emotional motivations driving purchase behavior. Use this data to develop a messaging strategy for key touch points in your customers' journeys.

Using results from Right Brain Research, we sort consumers into “types” of people, developing a segmentation of the market based on the underlying Emotional Motivations that drive purchase behavior.

This segmentation will not only provide a deeper understanding of a wider audience’s perception of a brand, it will also reveal the best opportunities for a brand within that wider audience and the best messaging elements to utilize for each segment.
The comprehensive cluster analysis of the completed surveys will reveal how the broader market is segmented emotionally, in terms of the Emotional Motivations and Barriers.

Emotional Segmentation serves as a vital tool when working with databases (or “Big Data”) for direct response marketing, social media or even mass media where specific content appeals to various segments. We can predict with sufficient accuracy the Emotional Segment that each person falls into or belongs to by using the data from variables for which we do have data.
