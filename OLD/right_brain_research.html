Right Brain Research

Right Brain Research uncovers the strongest emotional motivations and barriers of key audiences.
When developing brand strategy, an organization is best served to focus first on understanding its best customers. Since emotion and motivation are right brain phenomena, and the right brain communicates visually rather than logically or with language, a visual technique is a key part of any research methodology. That is why we developed Right Brain Research.
1
The Right Brain methodology focuses on emotion by separating rationalizations from what truly motivates people. It is easy to uncover information about the rational, left-brain reasons people give for why they do what they do, but the complete answer is lacking without understanding the emotional side of decision-making.
2
Right Brain Research is the only methodology that uses visualization to access the emotional mind. In Right Brain Interviews we have respondents visualize and relive critical decision points so that we gain understanding of the key emotional factors - the Emotional Motivations and Barriers - that influence how they make these decisions.
3
We interpret interview material within a psychological framework: our Taxonomy of Emotional Motivations and Barriers. Our approach to motivation explains and predicts decisions and behavior. We will present to you the what, the how, and the why, in ways that will change how you understand and perceive the challenges you face.
4
Right Brain Research focuses on uncovering and exploring the similarities among respondents, rather than the differences, as traditional market research does. This model allows us to work with small sample sizes. For example, with 11 respondents who share key characteristics, the confidence level of the results is 90 percent.
5
Right Brain Research reveals vital Emotional Motivations, unmet needs, and consumer language – all of which are critical to developing quantitative research surveys.
