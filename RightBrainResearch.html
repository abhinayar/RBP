Right Brain Research

Uncovering the strongest Emotional Motivations and Barriers of key audiences.

Right Brain Research uncovers the strongest Emotional Motivations and Barriers of key audiences. When developing brand strategy, an organization is best served to focus first on understanding its best customers.

We developed Right Brain Research using a visual technique as a key component for two reasons:
  • Emotion and motivation are right brain phenomena.
  • The right brain communicates visually rather than logically or with language.

We pioneered consumer psychology and emotional research in 1972 and have been the leaders in the field ever since. We offer our clients a distinctive competitive advantage, because the Right Brain Approach is the only path to uncovering the motivational information at the deepest level.
It is a unique methodology for the following reasons:
  • The Right Brain methodology focuses on emotion by separating rationalizations from what truly motivates people. It is easy to uncover information about the rational, left-brain reasons people give for why they do what they do, but the complete answer is lacking without understanding the emotional side of decision-making.
  • Right Brain Research uses visualization to access the emotional mind. In Right Brain Interviews we have respondents visualize and relive critical decision points so that we gain understanding of the key emotional factors - the Emotional Motivations and Barriers - that influence how they make these decisions.
  • We interpret interview material within a psychological framework: our Taxonomy of Emotional Motivations and Barriers. We will present to you the what, the how, and the why, in ways that will change how you understand and perceive the challenges you face.

Our research focuses on uncovering and exploring the similarities among respondents, rather than the differences, as traditional market research does. This model allows us to work with small sample sizes. For example, with 11 respondents who share key characteristics,
the confidence level of the results is 90 percent.

Right Brain Research not only reveals vital Emotional Motivations, but also unmet needs and consumer language – which are critical to developing quality quantitative research surveys.
