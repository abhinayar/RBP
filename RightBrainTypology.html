Right Brain Typology

Segmenting the market based on underlying Emotional Motivations

Using discoveries from Right Brain Research, we sort consumers into “types” of people, developing a segmentation of the market based on the underlying Emotional Motivations that drive decision-making behavior.

We analyze the results from this unique quantitative approach using a comprehensive cluster analysis which reveals how the broader market is segmented emotionally.

This typology not only provides a deeper understanding of a wider audience’s perception of a brand, it will also reveal the best opportunities for a brand within that wider audience and the best messaging elements to utilize for each segment.

Right Brain Typology serves as a vital tool when working with databases (or “Big Data”) for direct response marketing, social media or even mass media where specific content appeals to various segments. We can predict with sufficient accuracy the Right Brain Type that each person falls into or belongs to by using the data from variables for which we do have data.
